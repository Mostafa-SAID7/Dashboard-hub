# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users. We value the participation of each individual and want to ensure that everyone has a positive experience.

## Our Standards

Behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment includes:

- Respecting and including different opinions and perspectives
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and understanding with others
- Helping others learn and grow

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Disruptive photography or recording
- Sustained disruption of community spaces
- Threats of violence or violent language

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussion forums and chat channels
- In-person and virtual events
- Any other spaces managed by the project

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is committed to maintaining confidentiality regarding the reporter.

## Consequences

Depending on the severity of the violation, consequences may include:

- Private warning from the project team
- Public warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributions
- Other actions deemed appropriate by the project team
